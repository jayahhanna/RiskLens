Metric Guide

Volatility

Shows how widely returns fluctuate. Higher volatility means larger portfolio swings.

Value at Risk (VaR)

Estimates a one-day potential loss threshold at 95% confidence under normal conditions.

Concentration Risk

Measures dependence on the largest position. Higher concentration means less diversification.

Risk Score

Combines volatility, VaR, and concentration into a single 0-100 risk indicator.
