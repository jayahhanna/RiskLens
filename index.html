Metric Guide

Volatility

Volatility measures how much portfolio returns move up and down. Higher volatility means larger swings in value.

Value at Risk (VaR)

1-Day VaR (95%) estimates a one-day loss threshold that may be exceeded about 5% of the time under normal conditions.

Concentration Risk

Concentration risk shows how much the portfolio depends on its largest holding. Higher concentration means less diversification.

Risk Score

The risk score combines volatility, VaR, and concentration into a single 0-100 indicator for quick risk assessment.
